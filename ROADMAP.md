## Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks
- **Market data gaps**: Implement multiple data sources
- **System outages**: Redundant infrastructure, failover systems
- **Strategy drawdown**: Robust backtesting, risk limits

### Operational Risks
- **Resource constraints**: Hire additional staff if needed
- **Timeline slippage**: Agile methodology, regular reviews
- **Integration issues**: Early testing, vendor communication

### Business Risks
- **Regulatory changes**: Compliance team monitoring
- **Market conditions**: Adaptive strategy parameters
- **Competition**: Innovation focus, continuous improvement
